Workflow

How OpsPilot works

From alert to resolution in three intelligent steps — fully automated, always on.

01

Detect & Ingest

OpsPilot connects to your monitoring stack and ingests logs, metrics, and alerts in real time with zero-config setup.

02

Analyze & Correlate

The AI engine maps service dependencies, correlates anomalies across signals, and pinpoints the root cause automatically.

03

Recommend & Resolve

Get prioritized remediation playbooks, notify the right team, and track resolution — all from one intelligent copilot.
